General Principles of Membership Upgrades

üéØ Merit-Based Progression

Upgrades are based on academic qualifications, professional experience, CPD compliance, and contributions to the sector. Each application is evaluated on its merits.

üìö CPD Compliance

Members must maintain the required CPD hours for their current grade before being eligible for an upgrade. CPD ensures readiness for the responsibilities associated with higher membership grades.

üìã Documentation & Verification

Applications must include verifiable evidence of qualifications, experience, CPD, and professional achievements. The Membership Committee or Fellowship Committee reviews all documentation carefully.

‚è±Ô∏è Clear Timelines

Student ‚Üí Associate: 10 business days | Associate ‚Üí Full: 10‚Äì15 business days | Full ‚Üí Fellow: 3‚Äì4 weeks. Timelines ensure efficient processing while maintaining rigorous standards.

‚öñÔ∏è Ethical Standards

Members must adhere to CIET's Code of Conduct throughout the upgrade process. Non-compliance may result in delays or rejection of the upgrade application.

üì¢ Appeal Process

Members may appeal decisions by submitting a written request to the Membership Committee within 30 days of notification, ensuring fairness and transparency.
