Code of Conduct

Upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and ethical practice in education and training.

Our Commitment to Excellence

The Chartered Institute of Education and Training (CIET) upholds the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and ethical practice. Membership carries the responsibility to maintain these standards in all professional interactions, both within and outside the Institute.

All CIET members are expected to demonstrate exemplary conduct, uphold the reputation of the profession, and commit to lifelong learning. Compliance with the Code of Conduct is mandatory and forms a core criterion for membership retention, progression, and eligibility for leadership roles or fellowship.
