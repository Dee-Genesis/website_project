Begin your journey towards professional excellence in education and training

Apply to Become a Member or Certified Professional

The Chartered Institute of Education & Training (CIET) welcomes applications from qualified educators, trainers, scholars, and practitioners who are committed to advancing professional excellence in education and training. We invite individuals at all career stages to join our distinguished global community through membership and professional certification.

Application Form

Accepted formats: PDF, DOC, DOCX (Max size: 5MB)

Accepted formats: JPG, PNG (Max size: 2MB)

Upload certificates, transcripts, or diplomas (Max size: 10MB)

After Submission

Once your application is received, the CIET Admissions and Membership Committee will carefully review your credentials and respond in due time. Should additional information or documentation be required, our team will contact you directly to guide you through the process.

Successful applicants will receive an official Admission Letter containing comprehensive details on membership registration, certification pathways, and fee payment instructions.

Important Information

  • All information provided must be accurate, complete, verifiable, and authentic.
  • Applications are evaluated based on academic qualifications, professional experience, contributions to the field, and demonstrated commitment to educational excellence.
  • Submission of false, misleading, or unverifiable information will result in immediate disqualification and may affect future applications.
  • All applicant data is treated with the utmost confidentiality and managed in accordance with CIET's data protection and privacy policy.
  • Applicants are encouraged to review membership grade requirements and certification criteria before submitting their application to ensure eligibility.

Chartered Institute of Education & Training (CIET) is a professional body dedicated to advancing excellence in education and training professions worldwide.
