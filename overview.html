Membership Overview

A structured, comprehensive pathway designed to support education and training professionals at every stage of their careers.

Overview

The Chartered Institute of Education and Training (CIET) offers a structured, comprehensive membership program designed to support educators, trainers, and education professionals at every stage of their careers. CIET membership is not just a professional credential—it is a commitment to professional excellence, ethical practice, and lifelong learning.

Membership provides access to professional development, research, networking opportunities, advocacy, and recognition. Whether you are an emerging professional or a senior leader, CIET membership empowers you to excel and make an impact in the education and training sector.
