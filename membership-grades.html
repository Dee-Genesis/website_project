Membership Grades & Application Process

CIET offers four membership grades, each reflecting academic qualifications, professional experience, contributions to the sector, and CPD requirements. Each grade has a clear application process.

Student Member

Entry-Level Membership

Eligibility

  • Currently enrolled in a recognized diploma, undergraduate, or postgraduate program in education, training, or related fields.

Professional Experience

  • No prior professional experience required.

Benefits

  • Access to workshops, seminars, and learning resources.
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals.
  • Participation in networking events and student-focused forums.
  • Exposure to professional development, ethical standards, and career pathways.

Application Process

  1. Complete the online application form.
  2. Submit proof of enrollment in a recognized academic program.
  3. Provide valid identification.
  4. Pay the one-time Membership fee.
  5. Await approval from the CIET Membership Committee (5–7 business days).
Membership Fee: $100
CPD Requirement: Minimum 10 hours per year through workshops, mentorship, or approved learning activities.

Associate Member (ACIET)

Professional Recognition

Eligibility

  • Professionals with foundational qualifications and some professional experience.

Academic Qualification

  • Bachelor's degree in education, training, or related fields.

Professional Experience

  • At least 3 years of teaching, training, or education-related experience.

Benefits

  • Access to advanced professional development programs, certification courses, and resources.
  • Networking with peers and senior professionals.
  • Participation in CIET-led projects, initiatives, and committees.
  • Recognition as a qualified professional in the sector.

Application Process

  1. Complete the Associate Membership application form online.
  2. Submit academic certificates and proof of professional experience.
  3. Include a personal statement outlining commitment to education and training.
  4. Pay the Membership fee.
  5. Await approval from the Membership Committee (10 business days).
Membership Fee: $200
CPD Requirement: Minimum 20 hours per year through workshops, seminars, research, mentoring, or CIET projects.

Full Member (MCIET)

Established Professional Status

Eligibility

  • Professionals demonstrating high competence, ethical practice, and contribution to education or training.

Academic Qualification

  • Minimum Bachelor's degree; Master's or higher preferred.

Professional Experience

  • At least 8 years in education, training, or educational management roles.

Benefits

  • Full access to CIET's professional development programs, research, and resources.
  • Eligibility to serve on committees, advisory boards, and strategic initiatives.
  • Recognition in CIET directories and publications.
  • Opportunities to mentor junior members.
  • Priority access to leadership and career advancement programs.

Application Process

  1. Complete the Full Membership application form online.
  2. Submit academic qualifications, proof of professional experience, and references.
  3. Provide a portfolio or statement of professional achievements.
  4. Pay the Membership fee.
  5. Await approval from the Membership Committee (10–15 business days).
Membership Fee: $350
CPD Requirement: Minimum 30 hours per year, including workshops, seminars, online courses, research, publications, mentoring, and participation in CIET initiatives.

Fellow (FCIET)

Distinguished Leadership & Excellence

Eligibility

  • Senior professionals with outstanding contributions and leadership experience.

Academic Qualification

  • Advanced degree (Master's or PhD) in education, training, or related fields.

Professional Experience

  • Minimum of 15 years of distinguished experience in leadership, policy, research, or educational management.

Benefits

  • Recognition as a global leader in education and training.
  • Exclusive opportunities for policy advocacy, advisory roles, and thought leadership.
  • Priority access to strategic partnerships, research collaborations, and international initiatives.
  • Eligibility to mentor members across all grades and participate in governance.
  • Contribution to CIET publications, conferences, and global projects.

Application Process

  1. Complete the Fellowship application form online.
  2. Submit advanced degree certificates, portfolio of contributions, and references.
  3. Provide evidence of leadership, research, or policy contributions.
  4. Pay the Membership fee.
  5. Await rigorous review by the Fellowship Committee (3–4 weeks).
Membership Fee: $500
CPD Requirement: Minimum 40 hours per year, including leadership programs, policy projects, research, publications, mentoring, and international collaboration activities.
