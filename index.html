Welcome to Chartered Institute of Engineering & Technology (CIET)

The Chartered Institute of Engineering and Technology (CIET) is a professional body dedicated to advancing the discipline and practice of engineering excellence, ethical standards, and global recognition.

CIET serves as a global platform for professionals, researchers, and organizations committed to leveraging knowledge as a strategic asset for innovation, growth, and sustainable performance.

Why Choose CIET?

Career Advancement

We contribute not only to the growth of individual careers but also to the transformation of industries and communities across the globe.

Standard Syllabus

We develop frameworks and guidelines that ensure our members consistently deliver accurate, relevant, and reliable analytical insights.

Global Certificate

The Certificate Verification Guidelines allow employers, institutions, and individuals to authenticate any certificate awarded by CIET.

Our Mission

To advance professional competence through rigorous certification and assessment, promoting ethical practice and professional integrity across all engineering disciplines.

We foster continuous learning, build a global community of professionals, and contribute to the advancement of engineering knowledge and practice worldwide.

Diverse Community

We provide access to a global community of experts, professional development programs, certifications, research resources, networking opportunities, and governance participation.

Partnership

We partner with universities, corporations, and regulatory bodies to develop frameworks that align with global standards while reflecting the dynamic needs of modern enterprises.
