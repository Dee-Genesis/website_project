Continuous Professional Development (CPD)

Ensuring members maintain competence, enhance expertise, and stay current with global best practices in engineering and technology.

Commitment to Lifelong Learning

The Chartered Institute of Engineering and Technology (CIET) places a strong emphasis on Continuous Professional Development (CPD) as a core requirement for all members. CPD ensures that engineers, technologists, and technical professionals maintain competence, enhance expertise, and stay current with global best practices in engineering and technology.

All CIET members are expected to engage in regular CPD activities, document their participation, and demonstrate professional growth. CPD is also a key factor for membership upgrades and Fellowship eligibility.

Recording and Submission

CIET maintains a comprehensive CPD tracking system to ensure all members meet their professional development obligations.

  • Members are required to log all CPD activities in the CIET member portal throughout the year
  • Evidence of CPD (certificates, attendance records, project reports, publications, or mentoring logs) must be submitted annually
  • CPD records are reviewed by the Membership Committee to ensure compliance with requirements
